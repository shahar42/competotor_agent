Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2025

What We Collect

When you use Idea Validator, we collect:

  • Email address - To send you scan results and monitoring updates
  • Idea descriptions - To analyze and search for similar products
  • Optional images - If you upload sketches or photos of your concept
  • Feedback data - When you mark competitors as relevant or not

How We Use Your Data

  • To perform AI-powered market scans and send you competitor reports
  • To provide weekly monitoring updates (if subscribed)
  • To improve our AI matching algorithms based on your feedback
  • We never sell your data to third parties
  • We never share your ideas publicly or with competitors

Data Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We use the following services to operate Idea Validator:

  • Google Gemini AI - For analyzing ideas and matching competitors
  • Email provider - For sending scan results
  • Render - For hosting our application

These services have their own privacy policies and are GDPR-compliant.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your data
  • Deletion - Request permanent deletion of your account and data
  • Unsubscribe - Stop receiving emails at any time (link in every email)
  • Correction - Update or correct your information

Data Retention

We keep your data as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You can request deletion at any time.
